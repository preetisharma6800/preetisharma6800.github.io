The Taj Mahal


The Taj Mahal is one of the seventh wonders of the world.It is a white marble tomb situated in bombay agra.It is located on the banks of river Yamuna and is surrounded by a 280m2 garden.It was built by the 17th century Mughal Emperor shah jahan in the loving memory of the emperor beloved and honourable wife,Mumtaz Mahal.It took 22 years and the 22,000 workers to construct the Taj Mahal. He had cut off worker's hands because he did not want anyone to be build such a Taj mahal again.The main cause of the yellow color of the Taj Mahal is sulphur dioxide(so2).
